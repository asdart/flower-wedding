(Why Us)

We grow, source, and arrange flowers that transform wedding days into living memories.

From soil to ceremony, we cultivate relationships with local growers and select each stem at peak bloom. Our arrangements honor the rhythm of the seasons — peonies in late spring, dahlias in autumn, hellebores in winter — ensuring your wedding flowers feel rooted in the moment.

Locally grown, sustainably sourced blooms

Seasonal palettes tailored to your wedding month

Hand-conditioned stems for all-day freshness

(Our Story)

From greenhouse to grand celebration

We began as a small flower farm in the Hudson Valley, growing heritage roses and wildflowers. Today, our Brooklyn studio combines that grower's intuition with refined design — arranging weddings that feel as natural as the fields where our blooms begin.
